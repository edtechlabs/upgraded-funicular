Decimals and Place Value

Decimals are an extension of our number system to include numbers that are between whole numbers.

Place Value Chart

Understanding the place value positions:

  • Ones
  • Tenths (0.1)
  • Hundredths (0.01)
  • Thousandths (0.001)

Reading Decimals

Example: 0.75 is read as "seventy-five hundredths."

Converting Fractions to Decimals

Divide the numerator by the denominator.

Example: \( \frac{3}{4} = 0.75 \)

Comparing Decimals
